Block.. Blaster?


Hi. For this game, you will have to move using the arrow keys and space to shoot, avoiding red blocks (enemies) and collecting blue blocks (health and ammo). Keep an eye on your Health and Ammo, the more you have at the end, the higher your score.
Tip: line up the enemies and shoot.

-You are the Green Block
- Arrow Keys to move
- Space to shoot
- Avoid Red Blocks
- Collect Blue Blocks
- you shoot in whatever direction you were facing
- Wave changes every 15 Seconds bringing in more enemies

To Start or Pause Left Click the Battle Field
